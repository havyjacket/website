About Me

I was born in 1996 in the city of Atlanta and have lived in the state of Georgia my entire life. Residing in the north metro suburb of Alpharetta, I am very familiar with the area in and around Atlanta and the significant growth of the north metro region recently.

At Georgia Tech, students pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science must choose two "threads", which determine the information they will learn in their upper-level courses (e.g. operating systems, networking, computer graphics, machine learning, etc.). More information about threads can be found here. The threads I have chosen are Information Infonetworks and Artificial Intelligence. My expected graduation date is December 2018.

I have a tremendous passion for Computer Science and the extensive network of opportunities the field has to offer. Please check out some of my creations!
